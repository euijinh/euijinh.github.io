I am a graduate student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University.

My research interests lie in machine learning, representation learning, optimization, efficient training, mechanistic interpretability, sequence modeling, and speech/language modeling. I am particularly interested in developing algorithmic and systems-level methods that improve the scalability, efficiency, and reliability of modern neural networks.

## Research Interests

- Optimization for deep learning
- Efficient training and scalable learning algorithms
- Neural architecture design
- Sequence modeling
- Language and speech models
